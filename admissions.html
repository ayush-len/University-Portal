Application Timeline (Fall 2026)

1. Portfolio & Application Submission

Submit your high school transcripts, standardized test scores, and GitHub/Project portfolio via the GTU Application Portal.

2. Technical Assessment

Shortlisted candidates will be invited to complete a 90-minute online coding and logic assessment.

3. Faculty Interview

A brief 15-minute virtual interview with our engineering faculty to discuss your past projects and passion for technology.

4. Final Decision

Admission decisions and scholarship offers are released via the student portal.
