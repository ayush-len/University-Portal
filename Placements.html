Placement Procedure

1

Job Announcement Form (JAF)

Companies fill out the formal JAF containing job profiles and criteria. The Placement Cell makes this available to eligible students.

2

Pre-Placement Talks (PPT)

Organizations visit campus to conduct PPTs, interacting with students and providing insights into the company culture.

3

Resume Submission

Eligible students submit their verified resumes through the central placement portal for shortlisting by the recruiters.

4

Phase-I Interviews

Shortlisted candidates undergo technical and HR interviews on campus during designated placement phases.
