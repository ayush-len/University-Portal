Life at Global Tech

Smart Classrooms

Moving beyond traditional lectures. Our Tier-1 lecture halls are equipped with dual-projection, high-fidelity acoustic arrays, and localized high-speed intranets for seamless digital learning.

Residential Hostels

More than just a place to sleep. Our residential blocks feature high-speed LAN, common recreation rooms, 24/7 security, and collaborative study lounges optimized for late-night coding sessions.

Clubs & Societies

Join over 40 active organizations. From the Robotics Club and Google Developer Student Club to the Motorsports Team building fully functional electric race cars.

Our flagship Techno-Cultural Fest brings together over 15,000 students for 24-hour hackathons, robotics wars, esports tournaments, and headline musical performances.
